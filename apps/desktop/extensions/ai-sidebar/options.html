AI providers (bring your own keys)

Add keys for as many providers as you like, then pick a default for the sidebar. Keys are stored on your account (encrypted end-to-end when a vault passphrase is set — see below) and only ever sent to the provider you choose.

If set, keys are AES-256 encrypted in your browser before syncing — the cloud only stores ciphertext. You'll enter it to decrypt on other devices.
