Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

TronBrowser is built privacy-first. This policy covers both the tronbrowser.dev website and the TronBrowser browser.

The website (tronbrowser.dev)

  • Privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics via CrawlProof — aggregate page views only. No cookies are set, and no cross-site tracking or advertising pixels are used.
  • No personal data is collected or sold, and there are no ad networks.
  • Standard, short-lived server logs may record IP and user-agent for security and abuse prevention.

The browser

  • No telemetry by default. TronBrowser does not phone home. It is built on Ungoogled Chromium, which removes Google background connections.
  • Your data is yours. Bookmarks, history, and profiles stay on your device, or in a database you control (your own SQLite, or our optional managed cloud).
  • Bring your own AI keys. When you use the AI sidebar, requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured; your keys are stored locally on your device.
  • No ads, no sponsored tabs, no affiliate link injection.

Contact

Security or privacy questions: security@profullstack.com. TronBrowser is published by Profullstack, Inc.
